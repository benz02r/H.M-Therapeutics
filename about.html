About Me

Welcome! I’m Hanisha, the founder of HM Therapeutics. I am a dedicated Sports Massage Therapist with a passion for helping athletes achieve peak performance and recovery. After graduating in 2024 with a Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Biochemistry, I found my passion for anatomy. To add, having worked in the fitness industry since 2018 I decided to combine my two passions, fitness and anatomy. This led me to take a leap into the Sports Therapy industry. Ever since, I’ve focused on providing tailored care for combat athletes, particularly those in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

Throughout my career, I’ve had the privilege of working with athletes at all levels, from local gyms to high-stakes competitions. As well as working with people such as labourers and office workers to help reduce pain. My hands-on experience at sporting events has honed my skills in aiding recovery, reducing soreness, and improving mobility to keep athletes and people experiencing pain in general in top form.

Whether you're preparing for a big match, recovering from an injury, looking to optimize your training or simply seeking relaxation and pain relief, I’m here to support you every step of the way. With a deep understanding of the physical demands of combat sports, my goal is to help you move, perform, and feel your best.
